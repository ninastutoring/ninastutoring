About Me

Hi! My name is Nina and I'm a student at Stanford University studying Computer Science on the AI track. I'm excited about startups and all things entrepreneurship. As of June 2023, I'm currently a software engineering intern at a climate tech startup, and I'm a research assistant at Stanford Intelligent Systems Laboratory, where I'm working on improving the safety verification of self-driving cars. In my free time, I enjoy traveling, trying new foods, and surfing.

Why choose your services over other tutors?

I offer high quality tutoring and advising for more reasonable prices than existing college counseling and tutoring services. My personal experience with AP classes, standardized testing, finding extracurriculars, and building college applications allows me to empathize with the stress of academics and the college admissions process. My hope is to make your journey as painless and stress-free as possible. If you are curious, feel free to ask about my stats (GPA, SAT, AP tests, etc.) during the free consultation.

What are your qualifications?

  • 4+ years of 1:1 tutoring experience
  • I've read and provided thorough feedback for over 800+ college admissions essays.
  • Several students I've worked with have been accepted to their dream schools, including Harvard, Stanford, Brown, Northwestern, Columbia, and USC.
  • As a peer, I hope to be there as a mentor and guide more than just a tutor or advisor! See my testimonials below.

My Services

Tutoring

I tutor students grades 4th-12th. Tutoring may be focused on grades, AP test scores, or SAT/ACT prep. I'm currently offering tutoring for Algebra, Geometry, Trigonometry, Pre-Calculus, Calculus, Computer Science, English/Writing, and Statistics.

College Counselling

I will be a peer guiding you through the admissions process. This might include finding exciting extracurriculars that stand out, catalyzing passion projects, answering questions about the process, and finding the right college fit.

Essay Editing

No matter where you are in the writing process—whether you have a blank page or a final draft—I will help you brainstorm, discuss my feedback in depth, and equip with you the skills to write outstanding essays.
