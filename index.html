About Me

Hi! My name is Nina and I am a freshman at Stanford University. I plan to study Electrical Engineering or Computer Science. I decided to take a gap year due to the pandemic, and I spent the time tutoring, taking classes, interning at a startup in San Francisco, and teaching myself how to surf. In my free time, I can usually be found hanging out at the lake in Chicago or working on a passion project. I've been tutoring for 5 years now, and I have extensive experience with college admissions, AP classes, standardized testing, finding extracurriculars, editing college essays, and building applications.

Why choose my services over other tutors?

I offer competitive results for affordable prices. Also, I can empathize with the stress of academics and the college admissions process. My goal is to make your journey as painless and stress-free as possible.

What are my qualifications?

Primarily, five years of tutoring experience and acceptance into many competitive schools. I have successfully mastered the academic subjects that I tutor. My stats include 1530/1600 (99th percentile) on the SAT and 5/5 on several AP tests including Statistics, Calculus, and Chemistry. If it interests you, feel free to ask about my stats during the free consultation.

My Services

College Counseling

Help with finding (or creating your own) unqiue extracurriculars that you actually love doing, selecting high school courseloads, and finding the right college fit.

Academic Tutoring

I tutor students grades 6th-12th. Tutoring can be focused on grades and/or AP test scores. Subjects include Algebra, Geometry, Trigonometry, Calculus, English, Physics, Chemistry, Computer Science, and Statistics.

SAT and ACT prep

First, we will determine if the SAT or ACT is right for you. After choosing a test, I can help you reach your goal score and recommend the best free study resources.

College Essay Help

I can help you wherever you are in the writing process, from preliminary brainstorming to advanced writing techniques. I will also share my essays and what worked for me.

Coding

Don't know where to start learning programming? I can lead interactive coding exercises that will give you basic skills in computer science.

Mentorship

My sessions are not all about the academics. I'm here to give you confidence, peace of mind, and help ease your stress.
